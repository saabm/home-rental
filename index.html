Description

This beautifully remodeled San Jose home offers modern style and everyday convenience. Luxury vinyl plank flooring runs throughout, complemented by double-pane windows, recessed lighting, central heating, and air conditioning. The open living and dining area flows into a gourmet kitchen featuring a center island with a waterfall quartz countertop, stainless steel appliances, a gas range with an industrial-strength hood, a dishwasher, refrigerator, and ample cabinet space.

The remodeled hall bathroom includes double sinks and a stall shower, while the remodeled master bath adds a touch of luxury. Outside, the backyard boasts fruit trees (pomegranate, clementine, lemon, plums, cherries, almonds, peach, and kumquat), creating a peaceful retreat. An attached two-car garage provides secure parking and extra storage.

Conveniently located near highways 880, 280, and 87, this home is minutes from Valley Medical, Santana Row, Valley Fair, San Jose International Airport, and Downtown San Jose, and less than 5 miles or 15 minutes from major tech headquarters including Apple, Nvidia, Netflix, eBay, Adobe, PayPal, and Intel.
